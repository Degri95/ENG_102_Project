Description of Purpose

This website's purpose is to inform cybersecurity enthusiasts and professionals about AI's use in cybersecurity. The change to AI based systems has already began, and learning these tools and skills is necessary to maintain a career. By staying informed and knowing how to keep skills relevant to the industry, employees can stay valuable. Like many technological advances before, job roles will change, be removed, and new jobs will be created. Promoting continuous learning within the community is how we can stay relevant. Informing the audience that AI is a tool and we must learn to work with it.
